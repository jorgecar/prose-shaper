# Synaesthesia

**Definition.** Describing one sense in terms of another — *loud color*, *bitter silence*, *sharp green*.

**Example.**
- Wrong: The dashboard looks visually busy and overwhelming.
- Good: The dashboard is loud. You can hear the buttons shouting from across the page.
