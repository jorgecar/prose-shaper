# Diacope

**Definition.** A word or phrase repeated with a small interruption between the two occurrences — *X, [aside], X*.

**Example.**
- Wrong: We need speed — the kind that lets us iterate twice a week.
- Good: We need speed — real speed — the kind that lets us iterate twice a week.
