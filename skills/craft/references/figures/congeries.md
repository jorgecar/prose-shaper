# Congeries

**Definition.** A pile-up of items, adjectives, or phrases — accumulation past the point of necessity, until the sheer length of the list becomes the argument.

**Example.**
- Wrong: The launch had a lot of issues.
- Good: The launch broke auth, broke billing, broke the dashboard, broke the mobile app, broke the docs site, and — somehow — broke the status page that was supposed to tell people things were broken.
