# Merism

**Definition.** Naming the parts to evoke the whole — *body and soul*, *flesh and blood*, *from sea to shining sea*.

**Example.**
- Wrong: Everyone in the company, regardless of role, should attend.
- Good: From the intern who joined Monday to the VP who signed our last fundraise — everyone in the room.
