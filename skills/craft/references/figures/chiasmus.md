# Chiasmus

**Definition.** A reversed mirror — *A B, B A*. The second half inverts the order of the first.

**Example.**
- Wrong: We've been making engineers fit the deadlines when we should make the deadlines fit the engineers.
- Good: We have been shaping engineers to fit deadlines; let us shape deadlines to fit engineers.
