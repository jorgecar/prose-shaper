# Zeugma

**Definition.** A verb (or other governing word) is stated once but applies to several clauses or objects, often with different shades of meaning.

**Example.**
- Wrong: Tom prefers whisky. Dick prefers vodka. Harry prefers something stronger.
- Good: Tom drinks whisky, Dick vodka, Harry whatever the bar will sell him.
