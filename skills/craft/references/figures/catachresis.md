# Catachresis

**Definition.** A word deliberately misapplied — used in a context it does not literally fit, in a way that startles rather than confuses.

**Example.**
- Wrong: The CEO sharply criticized the team's strategy.
- Good: The CEO carved the team into thirds with a single sentence.
