# Adynaton

**Definition.** Describing the impossible as a way of saying *never* — *when pigs fly*, *when hell freezes over*.

**Example.**
- Wrong: We will never migrate to that platform.
- Good: We will migrate to that platform the week finance volunteers to do the data cleanup themselves.
