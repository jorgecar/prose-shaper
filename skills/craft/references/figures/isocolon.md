# Isocolon

**Definition.** Successive clauses of identical grammatical structure and roughly equal length.

**Example.**
- Wrong: The new platform reduces latency, and we've also seen improved reliability and lower cost.
- Good: Latency falls. Reliability climbs. Cost holds.
