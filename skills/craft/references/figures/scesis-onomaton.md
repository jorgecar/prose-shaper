# Scesis Onomaton

**Definition.** A string of nouns or short phrases with no main verb — verbless sentences that paint a scene through accumulation.

**Example.**
- Wrong: The state of the launch is uncertain because of open bugs, missing docs, and incomplete testing.
- Good: Launch day. Three open bugs. No docs. No QA sign-off.
