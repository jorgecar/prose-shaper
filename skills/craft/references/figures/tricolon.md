# Tricolon

**Definition.** Three parallel items, often with the third the longest or the surprising one.

**Example.**
- Wrong: It needs to be fast, reliable, and the team needs to be able to maintain it for the next five years.
- Good: Fast to write, fast to read, fast enough that no one has to defend it in review.
