# Parataxis

**Definition.** Short, sequential statements with no subordination — the prose of consequence, not explanation.

**Example.**
- Wrong: Although we shipped on time, the launch revealed three bugs we had not anticipated, which caused a regression in conversion.
- Good: We shipped on time. The launch revealed three bugs. Conversion dropped.
